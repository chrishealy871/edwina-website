About Me

Edwina has over 35 years national and international experience in health and social care services, across the public and private sectors. Edwina is driven by her passion to enable people to deliver quality services. Edwina has considerable deep experience, knowledge, commitment and reputation as a practitioner, educationalist and manager across corporate, clinical and social services. She has worked in large and small organisations, HSE, NHS, Canadian Hospitals and nursing homes.
