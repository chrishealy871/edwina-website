About Me

Edwina has over 35 years national and international experience in health and social care services, across the public and private sector. She is driven by her passion to enable people to deliver quality services. Edwina has considerable deep experience, knowledge, commitment and reputation as a practitioner, educationalist and manager across corporate, clinical and social services. Edwina has worked in large and small organisations, HSE, NHS, Canadian Hospitals and nursing homes Edwina was the first head of Quality and Risk in the HSE and subsequently went on to develop and manage the national Healthcare audit service similar to (Internal audit) for clinical and social services. Based on the acknowledged creditability of this experience people have the confidence and trust to engage with Edwina. In this, she encourages staff to think creatively and question ways of working and learning. She has an ability to identify where policy and procedure can be improved, engaging with staff at all levels to understand what standards and regulations mean in practice, to implement the changes and provide evidence of implementation. Uniquely, Edwina is prepared to get involved and Implement the changes necessary with the people on the ground, to build capability and the confidence in people to continue the changes and ways of working when her intervention is completed.
